07 — SMS

SMS is reserved for time-critical moments only — trial expiry and failed payment. One link, one action, opt-out on every commercial message.

Trial expiry + failed payment
In INCEDO
Thursday 12 Jun · 09:30
Incedo: Your Pro trial ends tomorrow, Fri 13 Jun. Keep your plan and pricing: inc.do/keep-pro
Today · 08:15
Incedo: We could not collect payment for invoice 2026-0136 (€1,240.00). Update your payment method: inc.do/pay/0136. Reply STOP to opt out.
Channel rules
Sender ID — INCEDO Registered alphanumeric sender, no reply-to number for marketing.
One message, one action ≤ 160 characters, a single short link, no follow-up sequences.
Triggers Trial T−1 day and failed payment only. Upgrades and offers never go to SMS.
Opt-out STOP keyword on commercial messages; payment failures are transactional.
